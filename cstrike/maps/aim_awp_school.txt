Description:
Each side is symmetrical, containing plenty 
of cover to hide behind and AWPs to pick up.
There is an invisible wall across the middle
so keep to your cover!

Terrorists:
Snipe the Counter-Terrorists before they snipe
you!

Snipe the Terrorists before they snipe you!

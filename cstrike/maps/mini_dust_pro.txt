Mini Dust Pro

The classic map of cS_mApPeR for Counter-Strike 1.6 remaked for CS: Source by Strial.
It's have two bombsides and a capacity for 20 players, ideal for zombies, deathmatch, gungame or normal playing.
Specials thanks to the creator of the original map, cS_mApPeR and for my friends kEEGAN and cAMELLO; :3 in the testing process.
I hope you enjoy my first map.
